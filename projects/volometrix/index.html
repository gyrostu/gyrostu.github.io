Design at Volometrix UX

Principles, guidelines and assets for designing and building awesome Volometrix experiences.

We challenged ourselves to create a visual language for our users that synthesizes the classic principles of good design with the innovation and possibility of technology and science. This is material design. This spec is a living document that will be updated as we continue to develop the tenets and specifics of material design.

Overview

Create a visual language that synthesizes classic principles of good design with the innovation and possibility of technology and science.Our Design PrinciplesOur product is about getting your work done as effectively as possible. Inspired by Bauhaus and its focus on functional design, we aim to provide just enough design to create an awesome experience. When in doubt, leave it out.
